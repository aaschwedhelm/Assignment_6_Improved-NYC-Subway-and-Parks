New York City Subway and Parks

A visualization of New York City subway stations, lines, and parks in all 5 NYC boroughs
.

Methodology

Data was downloaded from the "NYC Open Data" and "Bytes of the Big Apple" websites in shapefile format and converted to geojson format in QGIS. The subway lines layer was edited in QGIS to resemble the original NYC Subway map more closely. While editing the shapefile, the subway lines were moved slightly to keep them from overlapping

Data Sources:
Subway Lines and Stations: Downloaded from NYC Open Data; source: MTA
Community Districts and NYC Parks: Downloaded from Bytes of the Big Apple (NYC DCP); Community Districts source: NYC DCP. Parks source: NYC DoITT
